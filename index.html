01 / The Architect

Innovating the Future
of Digital Fashion.

With over 10 years of expertise, I specialize in replacing traditional fashion workflows with cutting-edge 3D, AI, and automation-driven solutions. My work focuses on transforming how fashion is designed, visualized, and delivered, bridging the gap between creative vision and technical execution while enabling speed, scalability, and precision across digital pipelines.

Currently, I lead Research & Development at Avataar.ai, where I architect and scale next-generation digital fashion systems. My role involves ensuring 3D garment assets are optimized and compatible across AR/VR platforms such as Snapchat, Meta, and Google AR. I have successfully spearheaded the transition from physical photoshoots to 3D Virtual Renders, achieving a 99.7% approval rating while significantly reducing production time and costs.

Beyond technology and product innovation, I am the Co-Founder of Integrity People Trust, a nonprofit initiative driven by the belief that education and access to technology can change lives. The trust is dedicated to empowering underprivileged youth through educational support, mentorship, digital literacy, and exposure to emerging technologies like AI and digital transformation.

Integrity People Trust envisions a future where talent is never limited by circumstance. By connecting students with mentors, enabling skill development, and opening pathways to modern careers, the trust aims to build a generation that is confident, skilled, and future-ready.
